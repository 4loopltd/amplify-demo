How to prove who you are

1. We will help you to send us digital photos of your ID documents.

2. We will help you to send us a digital photo for use on your new ID.

3. We will scan your face to check against your photo.

4. Finally, we will help you to make a short video.


You may want to ensure you have a quiet, private space to complete this process.

The face scanning technology we use involves flashing lights and colours.
